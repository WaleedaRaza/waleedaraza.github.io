PokerGeek

In Dev

Provably fair poker with analytics-native progression — play, improve, prove.

Problem

Online poker feels untrustworthy. Players can't verify fairness, training is disconnected from play, and there's no unified place to compete and improve.

Solution

PokerGeek combines provably fair gameplay with analytics-native progression. Every hand becomes data for improvement, with cryptographic proofs ensuring trust.

Unique Value Proposition

  • Cryptographic shuffle proofs visible to players
  • Analytics dashboard for every session
  • Private rooms with invite links
  • Progression system with ranks and achievements
  • Hand history with replay and annotation
  • Fair matchmaking based on skill
