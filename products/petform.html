Petform

In Dev

One home for pet profiles, reminders, health logs, and AI-powered care insights.

Problem

Pet care is fragmented. Owners track health in notes, texts, and scattered vet PDFs. They miss medications, forget appointments, and can't share access with family.

Solution

Petform is one home for pet life — profiles, reminders, records, AI insights, and shared access all in one place.

Unique Value Proposition

  • Detailed pet profiles with photos and history
  • Medication, feeding, and appointment reminders
  • Health event logging with symptoms
  • AI-powered care Q&A
  • Shared access with family and sitters
  • Vet record storage
