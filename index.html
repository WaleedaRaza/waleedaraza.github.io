[2] MIND · INPUT

▶ READING · MUSIC · ARTS

Taste as signal. Learning as system.

Crime and Punishment

Dostoevsky · Book

Psychology before it was clinical. Maps the architecture of justification, how we build narratives to survive our own actions.

Thinking in Systems

Donella Meadows · Book

The closest thing to a moral framework for engineers. Leverage points, feedback loops, unintended consequences.

The Laws of Human Nature

Robert Greene · Book

Either you understand people, or you get managed by people. Hidden patterns in human behavior: status, envy, irrationality.

The Road to Serfdom

F.A. Hayek · Book

I read across ideologies to model incentives. Hayek's warning about how good intentions can create systems that destroy freedom.

Heroin

Lana Del Rey · Lust for Life

Romantic nihilism with surgical honesty. Beauty as anesthesia, love as relapse, identity as an aesthetic. Not about drugs, about the shape of yearning.

All Apologies

Nirvana · In Utero

The purest kind of resignation: not weakness, not strength, just acceptance with teeth. Humility without surrender.

MTV Unplugged

Nirvana · Album

The last performance, stripped down. Candles, lilies, raw emotion. What's left when you remove the distortion.

YEEZUS

Kanye West · Album

Raw power + insecurity displayed as art. An X-ray of status hunger, control, and spectacle. Attention mechanics at full voltage.

Crystal Ship

Cigarettes After Sex

Ambient intimacy. The space between words matters more than the words themselves.

Man of War

Radiohead · OKNOTOK

A lost track that became essential. Anxiety as architecture, every layer builds tension until it collapses.

Private Music

Deftones · Album

I predict this is their final tour indicator. "I Think About You All The Time" and "Infinite Source" are farewells wrapped in distortion. Not for the algorithm, just for the moment.

Shutter Island

Scorsese · Film

Identity is a prison built by grief. How plausible it is to choose a comforting narrative over truth.

One Battle After Another

Documentary · Film

Watching for: what the story claims is 'winning,' what it punishes, what it rewards. Conflict reveals hidden rules.

Avatar 3

Cameron · Upcoming

Worldbuilding-as-politics: who gets resources, what 'progress' means, and how narratives justify domination.

Rewatched 2 Peak Animes

Attack on Titan · Jujutsu Kaisen

Eren and Gojo. Freedom as burden, power as isolation. Both shows understand that the strongest characters are the most trapped.
